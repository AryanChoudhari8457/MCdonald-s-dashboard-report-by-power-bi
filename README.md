# McDonald's Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze McDonald's sales performance across different cities, stores, products, payment methods, and time periods. The objective was to transform raw data into meaningful business insights and practice end-to-end dashboard development using Power BI.

> **Note:** The dataset used in this project was synthetically generated using Claude AI for educational and portfolio purposes. It does not represent actual McDonald's business data.

---

## Tools & Skills Used

* Power BI Desktop
* Power Query
* Data Modeling
* KPI Cards
* Interactive Visualizations
* Slicers and Filters
* Business Insight Generation

---

## Key Insights & Recommendations

**1. Mumbai generated the highest sales (₹7.1M).**
**Recommendation:** Identify the factors behind Mumbai's success and apply similar strategies in other cities to improve overall performance.

**2. UPI was the most preferred payment method.**
**Recommendation:** Introduce more UPI-based offers and rewards to encourage digital transactions.

**3. STR008 and STR007 recorded the highest average ratings (4.10).**
**Recommendation:** Adopt the service practices of these stores to enhance customer satisfaction across all outlets.

**4. Mall Road was the top-performing location (₹6.7M in sales).**
**Recommendation:** Analyze what drives this location's success and replicate those strategies at other locations.

**5. Big Mac was the best-selling item (₹7.14M), while Hash Browns had the lowest sales.**
**Recommendation:** Maintain sufficient Big Mac inventory and explore promotions to improve Hash Browns sales.

**6. May and June recorded the highest monthly sales volumes (₹2.7M each).**
**Recommendation:** Leverage the strategies and promotions used during these peak months to boost sales during slower periods.
